Privacy Policy – INFLYGNING

INFLYGNING is a flight simulator app.

The app uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob). These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Usage data (how the app is used)
  • Diagnostics and crash data

This data is used only to show ads, measure ad performance and improve the app.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.

You can control ad tracking in iOS:
Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
